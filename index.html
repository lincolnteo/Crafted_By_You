Crafted By You

Crafted By You was founded in 2022 with a simple purpose — to create meaningful, hands-on craft experiences for people and businesses.


We specialise in DIY craft workshops and have extensive experience handling corporate events, team-building sessions, birthday parties, product launches, and promotional activities for brands and banks. Our workshops are designed to be engaging, creative, and suitable for all skill levels.


At Crafted By You, we believe that creativity is more than an activity — it's a way to connect, unwind, and create moments that last beyond the workshop itself.
